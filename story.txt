Once upon a time, there was an adventurous boy. One day, he woke up and decided to go for a walk in the forest near his home. Suddenly, there was a loud clatter and the boy fell into a pit.

This pit lead to a tunnel. This boy wanted to get out, so he try to walk along the tunnel. Suddenly, a dragon flied to him and try to attack him. After a fierce bettle, the boy killed the dragon.

The boy came home with a beautiful teal dragon scale as proof of his bravery. He gave it to the princess whom he
loved to wear as a necklace and won her heart. They got married and lived happily ever after. To this day he continues to tell his children all the tales of his many adventures.
